01

Initialize Broth Compiler Running

Begin by initializing the pot with 500ml of water. Bring the system to a boil at 100°C. Insert the bone broth concentrate slowly to avoid overflow. Simmer on low thread priority for 10 minutes.

lightbulb

Pro Tip: Maintain constant temperature to emulsify fats properly.

02

Dependency Injection (Noodles)

Inject the wheat noodles into the boiling broth. Allow the process to run for exactly 3 minutes. Do not exceed the timeout limit or the texture will become buggy (soggy).

03

Assembly & Deployment

Transfer noodles and broth to the serving container. Apply the Soft Boiled Egg patch and configure the Nori Sheet layout. Drizzle Chili Oil for final UI polish.
